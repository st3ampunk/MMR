Our Origin

Moogambigai Metal Refineries, is a part of Jayachandran Groups of Companies (JCG)

which is a new age company, well equipped with semi-automated machineries and infrastructure to deliver high quality, customized 4ead, aluminum, zinc and copper alloy ingots, recycled plastic chips and granules & products, stationery and automotive batteries manufacturing, waste electrical and electronics recycling, waste cable scrap recycling, solder dross recycling etc and other recycling activities adding up and are geared to scale up production to meet the ever growing demand of its products.

We lead the
the recycle revolution

The well-built facilities are located at Mangalore, Karnataka and Coimbatore & Perundurai, Tamilnadu and has its collection centres at various cities all over the country.With over 750+employees and 7 manufacturing units, the group main focus is to recycle the waste in a sound and environment friendly manner in order to prevent it from being land filled or mishandled.
We are evolving to become a complete solution in scrap recycling operations, which is our main goal. We are authorised by the KSPCB and CPCB and are also ISO 9001, ISO 14001 and OHSAS certified in standards and quality.
